Who I am

I'm a final-year B.Tech Mechatronics and Automation student at Vellore Institute of Technology, Chennai (2022 -- 2026), working at the intersection of mechanical design, embedded control, and intelligent software.

My hands-on experience ranges from building a NASA HERC competition rover — including chassis design, powertrain development, and telemetry systems — to engineering autonomous drones for the ISRO Drone Challenge with GPS-guided navigation and topology mapping algorithms.

As part of Team Movis at VIT, I contribute daily to ROS-based sensor fusion, path planning, power management, and telemetry for competitive rover platforms.

I think in systems, build from first principles, and care about delivering hardware-software solutions that are robust, testable, and field-ready.
